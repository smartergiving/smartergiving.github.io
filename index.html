Open Experiments in Philanthropy

The Goal

Triple the amount of charitable giving in the US ($330M => $1T)

The Hypothesis

A lack of trust exists at all levels of charitable giving. Improve trust and you increase giving.

Projects

Open Templates

We want to help introduce the philanthropic community to the powerful world of open source development. We developed free website templates for both nonprofits and grantmakers with everything each of them need to establish a modern web presence. Most importantly, all code is hosted publicly, meaning anyone can contribute to site improvements or access the data the templates implicitly share. Grantmakers | Nonprofits

Open Data

Most machine-readable data in the charitable world is stuck behind paywalls. We're exploring new ways to gather and share data so developers can more easily experiment with new philanthropic models. The data currently powers all of our Dispatches and will soon be available via API. View Project

Donate.js

Technology allowing intelligent donation buttons to be placed anywhere a nonprofit story is told. View Project

Dispatch

Dispatches are curated lists of effective nonprofits. We find experienced philanthropists in given topics and use their insights to present users a digestible list of 10 nonprofits. View Project

Kyn

It's easy to follow and mimic the investments of for-profit investors. We asked ourselves why it was so hard to follow and mimic experienced philanthropists, and decided to build a philanthropy platform around the idea. View Project
